For an 8-hour Odoo hackathon, don't make all four target users independent. Instead, make one main administrator who manages the system and use RBAC (Role Based Access Control) to restrict pages. This is simpler, faster, and looks professional.

Financial Analyst	2 (Dashboard, Financial Reports)

This design fully covers the problem statement while keeping the project manageable within the hackathon time limit. It also demonstrates proper RBAC, business rule enforcement, and clear separation of responsibilities, which are likely to score well with judges.
